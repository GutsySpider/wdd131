About Me

Hi! My name is Tenisha Bowman and I am a crafting enthusiast. I have always been a crafty permission but my store journey started when my husband, Michael, bought me a cricut machine. I started off making hair bows for babies and toddlers and that grew to making shirts for kids. Now I make shirts for all ages and home decor with so many other options in between. I love sharing my knowledge of crafts and it is my favorite past time with my three children. Thank you for entrusting me with your crafting needs and thank you for shopping small.
