Site Name: Just Simply Bowman

This is the company Name of my wifes crafting business. This represents simple customization to everday things from the Bowman family.

Site Purpose

This site provides a public spaces to introduce my wifes business in an accessable and shareable way. This site will include a marketplace page where all of my wifes projects can be posted for purchase. I will also include and about us page to introduce herself to others and show people what crafting has done for her and how it helps her express her creativity. I will also create a custom orders form. This is the majority of what my wife does is make custom apparel for her customers.
